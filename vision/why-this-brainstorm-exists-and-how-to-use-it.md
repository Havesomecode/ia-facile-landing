# Why This Brainstorm Exists And How To Use It

This folder captures the current exploration state of the project.
It is intentionally provisional. Nothing here is a final product decision unless explicitly marked as such.

We may go deep on one thread at a time and temporarily lose track of the others.
These files preserve:

- what we currently believe
- what we are assuming
- what remains unresolved
- which threads are worth resuming later

The goal is to make future exploration resumable without relying on memory alone.
