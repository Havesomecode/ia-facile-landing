# Open Questions, Important Unknowns, And Next Exploration Threads

## Decisions Made So Far

These are the strongest current decisions or near-decisions:

- The project is in exploration and validation mode, not implementation mode.
- The landing page should function as a product-discovery tool.
- The emotional axis of the concept is likely reassurance.
- "AI made less intimidating and more everyday" is central to the vision.

## Important Unknowns

These still need focused exploration:

- who the first audience segment should be
- what exact offer the signup represents
- which channel to test first
- how to measure need beyond raw signups
- how personal or human the brand should feel
- whether the first product should be service-like, educational, or software-like

## Good Next Threads

Any of these would be a strong next deep dive:

- audience mapping
- offer design
- visual identity exploration
- landing page research goals and metrics
- trust and proof strategy
- acquisition channel hypotheses

## Resume Map

If we return later and need to restart quickly, begin from these anchors:

### Project definition

IA Facile is currently framed as a reassuring bridge for non-technical people who want to begin using AI without fear.

### Business goal

Use the landing page to learn whether there is enough demand and which form of offer produces real pull.

### Visitor goal

Feel understood, feel safe, and take a simple first step by signing up.

### Most important open question

What exactly is the person signing up for?
