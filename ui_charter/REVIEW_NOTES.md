# ui_charter Review Notes

## Iteration 1

### Current preference

`calm-public-service-clarity` is the leading direction.

### Why it is leading

- it feels more squared and organized
- it feels more specific
- it inspires more confidence
- its structure feels clearer and more intentional

### Secondary direction

`reassuring-editorial` remains convincing and worth keeping in comparison.

Its strengths are:

- strong typographic dignity
- calm editorial tone
- good adult positioning

### Direction currently rejected

`warm-modern-guidance` is not the preferred direction in its current form.

Main reasons:

- the fades weaken the clarity
- the rounded UI language feels too soft
- the overall result feels less aligned with the desired specificity

### Steering implications for the next pass

- lean more toward the `calm-public-service-clarity` grammar
- preserve some of the editorial dignity from `reassuring-editorial`
- avoid soft fades as a default atmosphere
- avoid rounded UI as the main surface language
- prefer squared, ruled, structured layouts
