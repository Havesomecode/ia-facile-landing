# Warm Modern Guidance

## Visual Thesis

This direction should feel like a warm, modern guide for adults who are curious about AI but still hesitant to start.

The experience should invite the visitor forward without pressure. It should feel welcoming, clear, and human, with enough contemporary energy to feel current but not so much polish that it becomes a generic startup page.

## What It Should Feel Like

- welcomed
- less alone
- less intimidated
- gently oriented toward a first step

## Composition Rules

- use clear but slightly more expressive rhythm than a standard editorial layout
- use one or two memorable signature layouts rather than repeated stacked modules
- allow gentle asymmetry and varied pacing
- keep the structure open and breathable

## Surface Rules

- use restrained softness
- round only selected moments, not every surface
- keep shadows minimal
- avoid toy-like pills or over-soft panels

## Typography Rules

- use a warm, modern, clear adult voice
- make the sans system carry the layout
- allow a more expressive accent voice only in measured moments

## Anti-Goals

- no repetitive card grids
- no over-systematized SaaS composition
- no over-rounding every panel
- no playful beginner-app aesthetic
- no generic friendly startup branding
